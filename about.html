About CJ’s General Store

CJ's General Store and More is a nostalgic journey back to simpler times — a curated space for music lovers, dreamers, creators, and collectors. Built by a Gen X’er for everyone who remembers Saturday mornings, cracked sidewalks, mix tapes, and neon lights.

Whether you're browsing the Sound Room, flipping through the Library, hanging in the Clubhouse, or just chilling at the Office, every corner is made with care. This isn't just a website — it's a time capsule.

We're proudly ad-free (except for Google’s touch), powered by passion, and constantly evolving. Welcome home.
